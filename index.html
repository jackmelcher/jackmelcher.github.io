Hi, my name is Jack Melcher.
I grew up playing video games and decided to pursue a career in the Games Industry.

While earning a B.S. in Computer Engineering, I started to learn game development with Unity and Content Creation. Currently, I am employed as a Game Tester for Experis Game Solutions,
performing Quality Assurance for Microsoft Xbox Game Studios titles.

In my spare time, I develop games in Unreal Engine 4 as a self-taught Game Developer.
I have developed a couple of solo projects as well as participated in a handful of game jams.

New games are constantly being published by other indie developers.
So occasionaly, I will record gameplay of indie games and showcase them on my YouTube channel.
